DISCOVER THE MISSION 

  This mission started with a simple question :
     "DOES KNOWING ABOUT MENSTRUATION ENOUGH OR DOES KNOWING ABOUT ITS 
            COMPLICATIONS MATTERS MORE ?"

In today's world, awareness about menstruation has increased, but the conversation often stops there. Mostly, people feel that telling the teenagers about their menarche is enough, that is the only fact that teens need to know rest they'll manage on their own. Again the discussion starts, "Is this enough ?" NOOOOOOO...
Hence, at 'CRIMSON INSIGHTS: THE MENSTRUAL WELLNESS', we believe that understanding menstruation is just the starting point. It's equally essential to acknowledge and address the various complications that can arise, from physical and emotional challenges to have effective self care tips. We're dedicated to empower women and individuals worldwide to take control of their menstrual health. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, supportive, and inclusive platform for individuals to navigate the complexities of menstrual complications.
