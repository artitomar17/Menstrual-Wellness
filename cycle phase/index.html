Meet Aurora – A Journey Through Her Menstrual Cycle

🌑 Menstrual Phase (Days 1–5)

Aurora wakes up feeling a bit sluggish and crampy. She knows it’s the first day of her period. She puts on her comfy clothes, warms up a hot water bottle, and spends the day journaling and doing light stretching yoga. Aurora respects her body’s need to slow down.

🌱 Follicular Phase (Days 6–13)

As the days pass, Aurora feels her energy returning. Her mind feels clear, and she’s ready to take on new projects. She starts doing creative work, joins a dance class, and eats nutrient-rich foods. She’s glowing with confidence.

🌸 Ovulation Phase (Days 14–16)

Aurora feels powerful and vibrant. Her skin glows, her social energy is high, and she feels more connected to others. She schedules important meetings and enjoys her peak energy. A healthy smoothie and a long walk make her day perfect.

🍂 Luteal Phase (Days 17–28)

As her body prepares for menstruation, Aurora notices her mood shifting. She gets more reflective and craves quiet time. She prioritizes rest, eats magnesium-rich foods, and practices gentle yoga. She listens to her body and treats herself kindly.
