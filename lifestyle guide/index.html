🌿 Lifestyle Guide for Your Cycle

Gentle habits to support your body and mind during menstruation

🧘‍♀️ Mind-Body Practices

  • Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
  • Guided meditation for 5 minutes
  • Journaling thoughts and emotions
  • Affirmation: "My body is wise and healing"

🍎 Diet & Nutrition

  • Iron-rich foods: spinach, lentils, dates, jaggery with milk, apples
  • Anti-inflammatory options: turmeric, ginger
  • Avoid: processed sugar, caffeine
  • Drink: chamomile or fennel tea

🚴‍♀️ Exercise & Movement

  • 15-min walk or gentle stretching
  • Yoga: Child’s Pose, Cat-Cow, Butterfly Pose
  • Avoid intense cardio or HIIT
  • Try foam rolling for hip and back relief

💼 Work & Productivity Hacks

  • Use Pomodoro technique for focus
  • Desk yoga and frequent breaks
  • Plan low-energy tasks on heavy days
  • Keep heating pad at workspace

🧘 Yoga Poses for Menstrual Relief

  • Child’s Pose (Balasana): Relieves back and hip tension
  • Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana): Eases abdominal cramps
  • Supine Twist (Supta Matsyendrasana): Supports digestion and reduces bloating
  • Reclining Bound Angle Pose (Supta Baddha Konasana): Promotes calm and eases pelvic tension
  • Legs-Up-The-Wall (Viparita Karani): Boosts circulation and relaxation

😌 Stress Management

  • Practice deep breathing for 3-5 minutes
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Engage in relaxing hobbies (drawing, music, etc.)
  • Avoid multitasking; focus on one thing at a time

🛌 Rest & Break Tips

  • Take 15-minute rest breaks every 2 hours
  • Use eye masks or essential oils to rest better
  • Listen to calming music or white noise
  • Ensure 7–9 hours of sleep each night

🩸 Sanitary Items Guidance

  • Choose what fits best: pads, tampons, menstrual cups
  • Change products every 4–6 hours to stay fresh
  • Track flow to adjust absorbency needs
  • Always carry extras in a small hygiene kit
